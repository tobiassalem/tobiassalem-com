Philosophy

24 May 2011

What is it that inspires us to greatness? It can be a good friend or loved one. It can be the words of an insightful teacher, the dream of reaching higher goals together, to conquer the challenges placed before us. Or it can simply be the beauty of a fallen leaf. Whatever it is for you, cherish it always; for how would our lives be without it?

Have you ever experienced that boundless emotion when inspiration flows through your very being. When time and space simply fades away and all your focus is on what is right front of you. I feel that's when you can really focus on your work and reach amazing results. Even though the future is always unpredictable, when you know in which direction you are moving, the details don't really matter. You know that it will be something good, something you can learn from. That's the true spirit of ambition, to always be open to exploration and new learning
